🦇 How it works

Everything saves on this device — no login. The loop: in 📚 Study, flip Studied on, bump the revision number each review, and flag ⚑ weak or A Anki. Click a topic for tips, structures, oral script & notes.

Tabs

  • 🎲 Quadruplets — the 38 exam draws, coloured by your progress; top shows how many you know well.
  • ✏️ Structures — every structure to draw, with a picture. Full = exact · Basic = key features.
  • 🃏 Flashcards — pick a deck, reveal, rate Bad/Good/Great. Scores only — doesn't touch your marks.
  • 📊 Stats — countdown, the two "Am I Cooked?" bars (📚 Studied + 🔁 Revised ×2), activity, flashcard report, advisor.
